# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Being respectful and constructive in communication.
- Accepting feedback gracefully.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and project.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discrimination of any kind.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct. They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and other contributions that violate these standards.

## Reporting

Report behavior concerns privately to maintainers.
